# Lindelof Space
```ad-note
title:
$X$ is said to be _lindelof_ if every open cover of $X$ has a finite subcover.
```

---
# Related Results
1) Regular + Lindelof $\implies$ Normal
2) Metrizable + Lindelof $\implies$ 2nd Countable 
3) 2nd countable $\implies$ Lindelof
